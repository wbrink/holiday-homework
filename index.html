"The Show About Nothing"

Seinfeld was created by Larry David and Jerry Seinfeld. The show first episode aired in 1989 and was met with lukeworm approval. The show eventually took off and made it through 9 seasons and 180 episodes. Known as "The Show About Nothing" it follows 4 friends going through their everyday life in New York City.
